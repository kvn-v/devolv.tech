Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 7, 2026

ZoneMobile (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the ZoneMobile mobile application (the “App”).

Important: ZoneMobile does not collect or track GPS or background location data in Version 1.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name (if provided)
  • Email address
  • User ID associated with your account

Usage Data

  • Punch-in and punch-out timestamps
  • Organization or job identifiers associated with punches
  • Core app interaction data

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Authentication-related identifiers

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide punch-in / punch-out functionality

  • Display time records to authorized users

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

3. Data Storage & Security

ZoneMobile uses secure third-party services (such as cloud databases and authentication providers) to store and process data. We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information; however, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

4. Data Sharing

  • Within your organization: Authorized administrators may view punch data.
  • Service providers: Trusted providers that help operate the App.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. Administrators may manage data in accordance with organizational policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

ZoneMobile is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
