Frequently asked

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by the store the app was purchased from, not by Devolv directly.

For iOS, request a refund through Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com. For Android, request through Google Play: Google Play refund help.

I found a bug, what should I include in my email?

To help us reproduce the issue quickly, please include:

• Device model (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro, Pixel 8)
• OS version (e.g. iOS 18.2, Android 14)
• App version (shown in the app's settings screen)
• Steps to reproduce the bug
• A screenshot or screen recording if possible

Can I request a feature?

Yes. We read every email. Send your idea to support@devolv.tech with the product name in the subject line. We can't promise every suggestion ships, but feature requests directly shape our roadmap.

How long until I get a reply?

Typical response time is under 2 business days during US hours. Complex bug reports may take longer while we reproduce and diagnose the issue. We'll keep you posted.

Do you offer phone support?

No. Email is the only support channel. This lets us keep a written record of every issue and ship fixes faster.
